# merge-iter

```scheme
(define (merge-iter k i j) 
  (cond ((and (<= i q) (<= j r)) 
          (let ((low1 (vector-ref vector i))
               (low2 (vector-ref vector j)))
            (if (<<? low1 low2) 
               (begin (vector-set! working-vector k low1)
                      (merge-iter (+ k 1) (+ i 1) j)) 
               (begin (vector-set! working-vector k low2)
                      (merge-iter (+ k 1) i (+ j 1))))))
        ((<= i q)
         (flush-remaining k i q))
        (else (flush-remaining k j r))))
```
